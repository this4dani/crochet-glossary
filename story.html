My Journey

Over the years, I've discovered that crochet isn't just about following patterns—it's about understanding the why behind every stitch. When patterns don't fit quite right or when you want to use different yarn, that's where the real artistry begins.

I'm building tools that bridge the gap between traditional craft wisdom and modern technology, helping fellow crocheters spend more time creating and less time calculating.
