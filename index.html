DANI'S Complete Crochet Resource Platform

Welcome to DANI'S comprehensive crochet glossary and pattern adjustment platform. Our interactive stitch dictionary features over 253 crochet terms including single crochet (sc), double crochet (dc), half double crochet (hdc), treble crochet (tr), slip stitch (sl st), chain stitch (ch), and hundreds more stitches and techniques.

Master crochet terminology with our US to UK converter: US single crochet equals UK double crochet, US double crochet equals UK treble crochet. Learn advanced stitches like front post double crochet (fpdc), back post double crochet (bpdc), popcorn stitch, bobble stitch, puff stitch, shell stitch, v-stitch, cluster stitch, crocodile stitch, alpine stitch, moss stitch, seed stitch, waffle stitch, basketweave, cables, and Tunisian crochet variations.

Perfect for amigurumi makers needing invisible decrease, magic ring, and color change techniques. Ideal for blanket crocheters working with granny squares, c2c (corner to corner), ripple patterns, chevron, and temperature blankets. Essential for garment makers calculating gauge, ease, pattern grading, and yarn substitution.

Crochet tools and features: stitch counters, row counters, gauge calculators, yarn weight converters (lace, fingering, sport, DK, worsted, aran, bulky, super bulky), hook size charts (metric and US sizes), pattern reading guides, abbreviation decoders, and tension square calculators.

Learn techniques: blocking methods, seaming, joining as you go (JAYG), continuous rounds, working in rows, foundation single crochet (fsc), foundation double crochet (fdc), adjustable ring, magic loop, yarn over (yo), yarn under (yu), working in back loop only (blo), front loop only (flo), third loop, and post stitches.

Common crochet problems solved: pattern adjustment for different sizes, calculating yarn requirements, substituting yarn weights, adjusting for gauge differences, converting between US and UK terms, understanding vintage patterns, reading charts and diagrams, fixing tension issues, preventing curling edges, and achieving straight edges.

Created by Ashley Danielle Caldwell (DANI) after 30 years of crocheting, teaching in ministry, creating for 5 children, and recognizing the need for modern pattern adjustment tools. Combining traditional fiber arts with technology for gauge calculations, pattern grading, and automated stitch counting.
