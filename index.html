From 8-Year-Old Beginner to Pattern Problem Solver

I started crocheting at 8 years old, and after 30 years of experience, I thought I'd mastered everything. But one frustration kept haunting me: spending hours rewriting entire patterns just to make simple adjustments.

My tech journey began in middle school when a teacher introduced me to computers. MySpace HTML became my obsession - I had to have the coolest page, which meant learning hex codes and web design. Little did I know this would lead to solving crochet problems decades later.

As an office manager in ministry, I serve church communities with both hands-on and technology solutions. Life is a hot mess, but I have Joy in the Lord - and I'm determined to build tools that help fellow crocheters spend more time creating and less time calculating.

Building the Future of Crochet

The Problem

After 30 years of crocheting, I still spend hours rewriting patterns for simple adjustments. Different yarn weight? Rewrite everything. Gauge slightly off? Start from scratch.

The Solution

I'm building a pattern adjustment API that automates the math, so crocheters can focus on creating instead of calculating. Because our time should be spent making beautiful things.

The Heart

Serving church communities with hands-on and technology solutions while building tools that make crochet accessible for everyone. Where traditional craft meets modern technology.

Let's Connect

Join me on this journey of bridging traditional craft with modern technology. Whether you're here for the crochet, the code, or the ministry heart behind it all.
