From Air Force Teacher to Pattern API

It started in middle school with an Air Force teacher who introduced me to computers. Something just clicked. By high school, I was playing with Python and random number games - pure magic when computers respond to your commands.

Then came the MySpace era. I had to have the coolest page, which meant learning HTML and becoming obsessed with hex codes. Little did I know that obsession would lead to building AI for crochet patterns decades later.

I was a horrible student who hated high school, so college computer science wasn't happening. But I couldn't stop playing with software. Every website builder? Tried it. Turns out, being curious and stubborn about figuring things out is better than any degree.

Building the Future of Crochet

The Problem

After 30 years of crocheting, I still spend hours rewriting entire patterns just to make simple adjustments. Different yarn weight? Rewrite everything. Gauge slightly off? Start from scratch.

The Solution

I'm building a pattern adjustment API that automates the math, so crocheters can focus on creating instead of calculating. Because our time should be spent making beautiful things, not rewriting patterns.

The Heart

Serving church communities with hands-on and technology solutions while building tools that make crochet more accessible for everyone. Life is a hot mess, but I have Joy in the Lord.

Let's Connect

Join me on this journey of bridging traditional craft with modern technology. Whether you're here for the crochet, the code, or the ministry heart behind it all.
