Introduction

Welcome to DANI'S! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our crochet resource website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, including pages visited, time spent, and general location data
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device type to optimize your experience
  • Usage Data: Which crochet stitches you search for and which pages you visit to improve our content

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: If you email us with questions about crochet techniques or our API
  • Newsletter Signup: Email address if you choose to join our pattern adjustment API waitlist
  • Comments or Feedback: Any suggestions you share about our crochet glossary or tools

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our crochet resources and stitch glossary
  • To understand which crochet content is most helpful to our community
  • To notify you about updates to our pattern adjustment API (if you subscribe)
  • To respond to your questions about crochet techniques and our tools
  • To analyze website traffic and optimize user experience

Google AdSense and Advertising

Important: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements that help support this free crochet resource.

How Google AdSense Works

  • Cookies: Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other websites
  • Personalized Ads: Ads may be tailored based on your interests (you can opt out of personalized ads)
  • Third-Party Advertisers: Ad companies may collect information to provide relevant ads

Your Advertising Choices

  • Visit Google Ad Settings to manage personalized ads
  • Use AboutAds.info to opt out of interest-based advertising
  • Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser settings

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences for our crochet glossary
  • Analyze website traffic with Google Analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Improve our crochet resources based on usage patterns

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve our site
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • Email Service Provider: If you subscribe to our API updates (we use reputable services)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure hosting with HTTPS encryption
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to any personal information we collect
  • No storage of sensitive financial information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Disable cookies in your browser

Children's Privacy

Our crochet resources are designed for all ages, but we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
